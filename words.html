Word of the Week

✨ Expand your Dzongkha vocabulary and embrace Bhutanese culture.

Discover a new Dzongkha word every week, learn its meaning, pronunciation, and connect with Bhutanese culture!

བཀྲི་ཤིས་བདེ་ལེགས

MEANING: "Tashi Delek" is a greeting in Bhutan that means "good luck," "blessings," "cheers," or "all the best"

དགའ་བ

MEANING: Happiness and joy
